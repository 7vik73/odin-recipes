Butter Naan

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour(Maida) - 2 cups
  • Baking Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Baking Soda - 1/4 tsp
  • Sugar - 1tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Curd(Yogurt) - 2 tsp
  • Milk - 1/4 tsp(warm,adjust as needed)
  • Oil or Ghee - 1 tsp
  • Butter - for Brushing
  • Optional: Migella Seeds or sesame seed for topping

Steps

  1. Make dough: In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, soda, sugar, salt. Add yogurt, oil, and gradually warm milk to make soft dough.
  2. Rest: Cover and rest for 2–3 hrs (important for softness).
  3. Divide & roll: Divide dough into balls, roll into oval/round shape (not too thin).
  4. Cook on tawa: Heat iron tawa. Wet one side of naan with water, stick it to tawa (wet side down). Cover and cook till bubbles form, flip tawa upside down over flame to cook top directly.
    • (Or bake in oven at 250°C preheated, for 5–6 mins.)
  5. Finish: Remove, brush immediately with butter.
