Paneer Butter Masala (Curry)

Ingredients

  • Paneer - 200-250g(cubed)
  • Butter - 2 tsp
  • Oil - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 2 medium(finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes - 3 Large(pureed)
  • Cashews - 10-12(soaked and ground into paste)
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chili - 1 (slit, optional)
  • Fresh cream - 2-3 tbsp
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) - 1 tsp
  • Spices:
    • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
    • Turmeric - ¼ tsp
    • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
    • Garam masala - ½ tsp
    • Salt - to taste<-li>
  • Water - thaginantha
  • Coriander Leaves - for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare base: Heat oil + 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook till raw smell leaves.
  2. Add tomatoes: Mix in tomato puree, cook till oil separates.
  3. Add spices: Add chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, salt. Cook a minute.
  4. Cashew paste: Stir in cashew paste, cook 2-3 mins.
  5. Simmer gravy: Add ½-1 cup water, simmer 5-7 mins till thick and smooth.
  6. Paneer time: Add paneer cubes, kasuri methi (crushed between palms), simmer 3-4 mins.
  7. Finish: Stir in cream + remaining butter. Garnish with coriander.
